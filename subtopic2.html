AI as a Scaffold for Critical Thinking

CSIT 100 • Webography • Dina Ntoufas • 24 November 2025

Source A
URLBMC Psychology
Site authorZhang, Tian, & Lu
AbstractThis study analyzes how generative AI supports higher-order thinking and problem-solving in pre-service teachers. It shows that AI can enhance critical thinking when used to guide reasoning and reflection. The authors argue that AI tools can scaffold learning by offering personalized feedback and promoting metacognitive awareness.
Source B
URLEducation Next
Site authorMichael Godsey
AbstractGodsey emphasizes that AI can support critical thinking when students are taught to use it ethically and reflectively. He advocates for structured assignments that require reasoning and evaluation of AI-generated content. This approach helps students develop independent thinking and analytical skills.
