AI and Overreliance Risks

CSIT 100 • Webography • Dina Ntoufas • 24 November 2025

Source A
URLEducation Next
Site authorAndrew Kotran
AbstractThis article explores how AI tools may reduce students’ cognitive effort by offering quick solutions. It warns that overreliance on AI can undermine intellectual resilience and discourage deep engagement with challenging material. The author emphasizes the importance of struggle in learning and cautions against using AI as a shortcut that bypasses critical thinking.

URLWestern Michigan University
Site authorTeaching & Learning Office
AbstractThis resource outlines how AI can make learning faster but less reflective. It discusses the cognitive risks of students skipping mental processes when AI provides instant answers. The article highlights the need for educators to guide students in using AI responsibly to preserve critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
